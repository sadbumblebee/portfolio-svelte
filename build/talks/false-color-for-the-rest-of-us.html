False color for the rest of us

“Visualizing raster data usually means more bands, more problems. With availability of multi- and hyper-spectral imagery, just how do we represent the data to a casual audience at first glance? We'll learn novel techniques to show change over time in night sky data, flooding extents with SAR, and land subsidence with InSAR.”
